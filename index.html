Research

My work focuses on understanding how alloys degrade in aggressive high-temperature syngas environments, especially under conditions that promote oxidation, carburization, coking, and metal dusting.

Syngas Corrosion

Studying alloy degradation in CO-H₂-rich environments relevant to hydrogen and energy systems.

Metal Dusting

Investigating carbon activity, oxide breakdown, coke formation, and pit initiation mechanisms.

Alloy Design

Exploring Cr-rich and high-entropy alloys for improved resistance in carburizing environments.
